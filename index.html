About us

A great place to receive care

Bethphage Medical Centre Limited (B.M.C) was founded by Dr. Monday Robert Ntuk, a family physician, on the 10th of October 2010. The Hospital has grown continually due to the total commitment and dedication of the staff and the high level of reputation and goodwill experienced from satisfied patients. Our main goal is to provide acceptable, accesible and affordable technologically driven Family Health Care services while upholding ethical standards. We put into great consideration every member of the family.

For this reason, if quality care is your desire, we will be glad to offer you our services.
